Welcome to the Official Travel website for Los Angeles!
Los Angeles is a sprawling Southern California city and the center of the nation’s film and television industry. Near its iconic Hollywood sign, studios such as Paramount Pictures, Universal and Warner Brothers offer behind-the-scenes tours. On Hollywood Boulevard, TCL Chinese Theatre displays celebrities’ hand- and footprints, the Walk of Fame honors thousands of luminaries and vendors sell maps to stars’ homes. Home for the best beaches in the world , the best hotels and resorts. And towards the locations of the biggest companies in the world such as Apple and Google in Sillicon Valley.
